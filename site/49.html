Overview

Notebook 49 synthesizes the earlier notebooks into a reusable design method. Instead of optimizing a single point first, it ranks architectures by the size, connectivity, and robustness of their admissible operating regions.

The practical research question becomes: which design expands the connected region before selecting the final operating point?
