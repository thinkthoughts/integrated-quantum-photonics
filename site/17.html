Overview

Notebook 13 showed how nonlinear optics produces squeezed signal–idler pairs. Notebook 17 asks how those pair resources combine into larger entangled structures.

The central shift is from isolated correlations to graph organization: frequency modes become vertices, squeezing relations become edges, and the resulting graph becomes the resource for scalable continuous-variable quantum computation.
