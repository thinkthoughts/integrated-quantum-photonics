Overview

Notebook 29 treated loss as a design constraint. Notebook 31 asks how that constraint is measured, localized, and converted into an engineering loop.

The goal is to move from uniform resource allocation toward specification-driven allocation: measure the admissible region, identify critical paths, and prioritize the improvements that preserve the usable photonic resource.
