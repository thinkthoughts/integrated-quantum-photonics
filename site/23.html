Integrated Photonic Circuits

Large entangled frequency graphs become useful quantum processors when integrated into cluster-state photonic circuits and operated through measurement-based quantum computation.

Overview

Many-mode entanglement provides the resource, but quantum computation requires an architecture that prepares, routes, measures, and reuses those optical modes efficiently.

Integrated photonic circuits combine frequency-comb generation, cluster-state construction, and measurement-based quantum computation onto scalable chip platforms.
