Error-Correction Thresholds

Fault tolerance begins before the final operating point is chosen. This notebook treats thresholds as boundary conditions on admissible regions, robustness margins, architecture choices, and engineering allocation.

Overview

Notebook 37 identified operating regimes. Notebook 43 asks whether those regimes remain large, connected, and robust enough to support fault-tolerant computation.

The central move is region-first: evaluate the admissible region, its connected component, its distance to failure, and its architecture score before selecting a final operating point.
